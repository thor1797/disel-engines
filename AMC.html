What is an AMC?

An Annual Maintenance Contract keeps your heavy diesel and industrial machinery under constant expert vigilance throughout the year — moving you from reactive repairs to planned, predictable maintenance.

Scope of Support

What's included

Every AMC plan covers these core pillars — delivered by our certified field engineers.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled rigorous physical inspections to trace micro-anomalies before they trigger system failure.

Performance Monitoring

Continuous parameter profiling of output stress, fuel delivery accuracy, and thermal limits.

Emergency Support

Ultra-priority fast dispatch. AMC contracts move your escalations to the front of response routes.

Load Testing

Regular on-site load bank testing to validate generator output under real operating conditions.

Spare Parts Supply

Genuine OEM spares sourced and supplied fast — stocked and ready to minimise any repair window.

Service Reporting

Detailed post-visit reports with observations, actions taken, and next scheduled maintenance date.
